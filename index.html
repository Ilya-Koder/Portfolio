About me

Originally from a small fjord in the northwest of Norway, I am a 25-year-old male who enjoys gaming and playing the trumpet in my free time. I discovered my passion for working with my hands at the age of 17 and became skilled in fixing and creating things, which led me to become a CNC Machinist/Operator. After working in the field for a few years, I faced a medical condition that set me on a journey to recovery. Though the cause remains unclear, I have learned to manage it better. During this time, I pursued my general competency education and began studying frontend development. I have always been fascinated by tinkering and discovered a love for website creation and coding, especially with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My knowledge continues to grow as I advance in my development career.
